# EVE ProgNative Ticket Workflow

1. Navigate to the New BOAT Ticket Form (NBTF) and set the "Channel" to either "ProgNativeDisplay" or "ProgNativeVideo".
2. Launch EVE (EPreP) and confirm your BOAT ticket inputs.
3. Select "Create New Asset".
4. For the "Main Image", upload a 1200x627 asset adhering to the standard 13-parameter tax naming convention.
5. For the "Logo", upload an appropriate asset (does not require strict taxonomy naming).
6. Depending on the platform (DV360 vs Yahoo), provide the optional/required "Square Image", "Mobile Image", or "Mobile Image Small" assets.
7. Fill in the "Advertiser Name" (e.g. Disney+).
8. Fill in the "Headline" (or "Long Headline") and the "Body" (or "Long Body") copy.
9. Specify the "Call To Action" (e.g., "Sign Up Now") and "Caption URL".
10. Click "BUILD PLATFORM DATA" and proceed through EDOT. Creative sizes should surface as 1x1s in the CM360 section of EDOT.
11. Click "MARK AS READY TO TRAFFIC".
