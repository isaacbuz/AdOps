# ESPN+ MLP Launch Process

1. Product Marketing creates MLP in Beta.
2. Read the ticket submitted to Ad Ops.
3. QA validate tags are firing in Beta.
4. Push changes to Staging environment.
5. Notify Product Marketing that Beta QA is complete.
6. Submit ticket to Web QA Team to perform Beta QA.
7. Wait for Web QA Team to complete Beta QA and notify Product Marketing.
8. If Disney is mentioned, ensure Product Marketing submits to READ for Beta QA.
9. Wait for Product Marketing to publish MLP to Go Live.
10. Upon go-live notification, validate in production that tags are firing.
11. Notify Product Marketing that tags are firing in production.
