# D+ Splash Page & MLP Ad Ops Workflow

1. Performance Marketing creates splash/MLP in Editorial-Preview.
2. Read the ticket submitted to Ad Ops.
3. Create rules & tags in Adobe Launch.
4. QA validate tags are firing in Editorial-Preview.
5. Notify Performance Marketing that Editorial-Preview QA is complete.
6. Performance Marketing submits READ request.
7. Wait for READ (Jason Dorn) approval.
8. Wait for Content Management to push changes to production.
9. Upon notification that splash/MLP went live, validate in production that tags are firing.
10. Notify Performance Marketing that tags are firing in production.
