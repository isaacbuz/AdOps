# EVE DV360 and Yahoo Ticket Workflow

1. Click the "Send to BOAT" button in the #4 base or create a net-new ticket in the New BOAT Ticket Form (NBTF).
2. Set the "Platform" to DV360 or Yahoo.
3. Select the target audience in "Audience Tactic(s) [Reporter-ID'ed]" (e.g. "DV360 - A18+" or "Yahoo - A18+").
4. Select the "Campaign Name" to auto-populate Content Type, Brand, and Title. 
5. Upload the creative assets. Ensure the taxonomy of the assets exactly matches the 13-parameter guidelines.
6. Submit the ticket and change the BOAT ticket Status from "Backlogged" to "In Queue".
7. Click "Launch EVE" to open the EVE Pre-trafficking Page (EPreP).
8. Verify Airtable parameters, confirm any platform override IDs (e.g., CM360 Campaign ID), and click "BUILD PLATFORM DATA".
9. Review EVE's Dynamic Object Tree (EDOT) and click "Mark as Ready to Traffic".
10. Once the Trafficker completes the ticket (Status "Done"), review the Trafficking Output Receipt (TOR) and QA the DSP end results.
